## Hi there 👋 I'm Ibrahim Hamza Maishanu

### Accelerator Diagnostics | Beam Instrumentation | Optical Simulation | Physics-Informed ML

I am an **M.Sc. Experimental Physics graduate** with research experience in **accelerator physics, beam diagnostics, and optical instrumentation**. My work focuses on **longitudinal beam diagnostics**, **Cherenkov and synchrotron-radiation-based measurement systems**, **streak camera image analysis**, and **simulation of accelerator diagnostic systems** using tools such as **Python, Geant4 and Xsuite**.

I have worked on projects involving:

* **beam pulse and bunch length measurements** using streak camera diagnostics,
* **analysis of synchrotron radiation and Cherenkov radiation diagnostics**,
* **Geant4 optical modelling of aerogel Cherenkov beam diagnostics**,
* **beam dynamics and accelerator modelling**,
* **signal extraction and image analysis for accelerator experiments**,
* and growing interests in **machine learning for scientific and accelerator applications**.

I’m especially interested in research at the intersection of:

* **accelerator instrumentation**
* **beam diagnostics**
* **computational physics**
* **optical simulation**
* **scientific machine learning**
